History

The first college of what is now SRMIST, SRM (Sri Ramaswamy Memorial) Engineering College, was established in 1985, followed by the other SRM colleges from 1992 to 1997.[3] The institute gained deemed status in 2002 as SRM Institute of Science and Technology and renamed SRM University in 2006.[3] In 2017, its name was reverted to SRM Institute of Science and Technology following UGC's request to drop "University" from the name

CAMPUSIS

Located about 35 km (22 mi)[5] away from the city of Chennai, this campus is situated on a 250 acre[6] site overlooking Grand Southern Trunk Road (GST Road), NH-32. The main campus at Kattankulathur consists of a College of Engineering and Technology, College of Medicine and Health Sciences, College of Science and Humanities, School of Management and School of Law. It is also one of the few schools in India that offers a Degree in Telecom studies

NCR Campus

The 25-acre (10 ha) campus at Modinagar on Delhi-Meerut highway houses the faculties of Engineering & Technology, Management and Teacher Education
